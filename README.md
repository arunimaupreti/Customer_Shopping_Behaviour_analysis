# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behaviour Analysis  

## 📌 Overview  
This project analyzes customer shopping patterns using SQL, Python, and Power BI to extract actionable business insights.  
It focuses on understanding customer segments, purchase behavior, revenue drivers, and product performance.  

---

## 🎯 Objective  
- Identify key factors influencing customer purchases  
- Analyze revenue distribution across demographics  
- Understand the impact of discounts, subscriptions, and shipping types  
- Enable data-driven decision-making for e-commerce businesses  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack  
- SQL (PostgreSQL) – Data querying & transformation  
- Python (Pandas, NumPy) – Data analysis (Jupyter Notebook)  
- Power BI – Interactive dashboard & visualization  

---

## 📊 Key Analysis Performed  
- Revenue comparison by gender  
- Impact of discounts on purchase behavior  
- Top-performing products based on average ratings  
- Comparison of Standard vs Express shipping spend  
- Analysis of subscription vs non-subscription revenue  
- Identification of high discount-driven products  
- Customer segmentation into New, Returning, Loyal users  
- Revenue contribution by age group  

(SQL queries implemented:)

---

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights  
- Revenue KPI & Total Sales Overview  
- Funnel Analysis (View → Cart → Purchase)  
- Category-wise Revenue Distribution  
- Customer Segmentation Insights  
- Daily Purchase Trends  
- Top Products & High-Value Customers  

---

## 💡 Key Insights  
- Subscription-based customers contribute significantly higher revenue  
- Discounts increase conversions but may impact profit margins  
- Loyal customers (repeat buyers) drive consistent revenue  
- Certain product categories dominate in both sales and ratings  

---

## 🚀 Outcome  
- Built an end-to-end data analytics pipeline from raw data → SQL → dashboard  
- Generated insights to optimize marketing, pricing, and customer retention strategies
